Architecture
How It Works
01
LSTM.py — Model Training
Downloads 15yr history, preprocesses with MinMaxScaler, trains stacked LSTM (100 units × 2 layers, Dropout 0.2), saves keras_model.keras.
02
flask_app.py — Dashboard
Loads trained model, fetches live data from yfinance & NewsAPI, renders all charts, financials, sentiment, and trade signal.
03
yfinance — Market Data
Free Yahoo Finance API. Provides price history, company info, income statements, balance sheets, cash flow — no key needed.
04
NewsAPI — Sentiment Engine
Free tier gives 100 requests/day. Fetches latest English articles per ticker. TextBlob scores polarity −1 to +1.
05
Technical Indicators — Built-in
RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Moving Averages all computed from pandas/numpy — no paid data feed required.
06
Sector Engine — Auto-grouped
6 sectors hardcoded with representative tickers. YTD performance fetched live with a single yf.download() call and cached 30 min.
